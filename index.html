How the Engine Works

💡 When a bank sends a transfer instruction, the mPOS engine runs a sequence of checks automatically — in under a second. No human involvement. No manual routing. The engine decides everything.
1️⃣
Bank sends instructionThe bank authenticates its customer and sends a structured API instruction to the mPOS engine.
2️⃣
Engine validatesAML screening, tier limits, FX rate lock, and transaction reference — all automatic.
3️⃣
Agents assigned randomlyEngine scans qualified agents on both sides and randomly assigns one each. Neither party can choose the other.
4️⃣
Local payouts coordinatedSender agent receives fiat locally. Recipient agent pays recipient locally. No money crosses the border.
5️⃣
Ledger balanced digitallyThe international accounting ledger records the obligations on both sides. Settlement is complete.

Who Are Agents?

👤 Agents are ordinary bank customers — not employees, not external companies. They have consented to participate in the mPOS network and have available liquidity. They earn a share of the fee on every transaction they facilitate. The engine assigns them randomly — the sender never knows who their agent is, and the agent never knows who the sender is.

Nigeria Agents 🇳🇬

💡 For the sender side, the engine checks the agent's international ledger balance — this is what facilitates the transaction. The ledger is a digital accounting record in their own local currency. It is not a separate currency or token.

UK Agents 🇬🇧

💡 For the recipient side, the engine checks the agent's local GBP fiat liquidity — because they need actual GBP to pay the recipient locally inside the UK banking system.

How Agent Conversion Works

💡 After facilitating a transaction, an agent holds an international ledger balance. They can convert this back to local fiat at any time — immediately if they want, or within 3 days maximum. They exchange with another agent in the same country only. This keeps the accounting ledger circulating actively across the network.

What is the International Ledger?

💡 Think of the ledger like a shared scorecard. When Nigeria sends to the UK, the scorecard records the obligation digitally — no money physically moves across the border. The ledger balance is denominated in each agent's own local currency. It is not a separate currency, not a token, not a reserve pool. It is simply an internal accounting record that keeps the system balanced.
✅ The ledger value never leaves the country. It is consumed as transactions are facilitated and must be replenished. All ledger entries are permanent records.
