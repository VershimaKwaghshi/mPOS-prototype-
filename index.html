Why Conversion Matters

⚖️ The 3-day conversion rule exists to keep ledger value in active circulation — not to penalise agents. Agents who convert promptly earn 0.05% on their conversion. The auto-conversion at 0.08% protects the corridor network from stalling when agents delay. The collective network benefits when all agents convert actively.

What is the International Ledger?

💡 The international ledger is an internal accounting record — not a separate currency or token. Ledger balances are denominated in the agent's own local currency. The value never leaves the country. It is consumed as transactions are facilitated and must be replenished. Ledger entries are permanent records — balances change as transactions occur.
